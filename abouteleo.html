About Eleo

Connecting Talent with Opportunity

Eleo is a platform dedicated to helping talented fashion creators become more visible and accessible to customers. We believe that skill deserves recognition and that talented creators should have opportunities to showcase their work beyond their immediate communities.

Whether you're looking for a tailor, dressmaker, designer, or fashion creator, Eleo helps you discover and connect with the right people.

Why We Started

Many talented fashion creators produce excellent work but struggle to reach new customers.

At the same time, customers often find it difficult to discover skilled creators they can trust.

Eleo was created to bridge this gap. Our goal is to make fashion talent easier to find, easier to showcase, and easier to support.

Our Mission

To create a platform where fashion creators can showcase their skills, grow their businesses, and connect with customers who value quality craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To become a trusted fashion discovery platform that empowers creators and connects communities through fashion.

What We Value

Visibility

Every creator deserves the opportunity to be seen.

Opportunity

Talent should not be limited by location or lack of exposure.

Community

Fashion creators and customers grow stronger when connected.

Growth

We support creators in building sustainable businesses.

Who Can Join Eleo?

Eleo welcomes diverse professionals across the fashion industry landscape:

  • Tailors
  • Fashion designers
  • Dressmakers
  • Bespoke clothing creators
  • Fashion entrepreneurs
  • Emerging fashion brands

Looking Ahead

This is only the beginning.

As Eleo grows, we aim to provide more opportunities for creators to showcase their work, reach customers, and build successful businesses.

Founder's Note

Eleo began with a simple belief: talent deserves visibility.

Across our communities are skilled tailors, designers, and fashion creators whose work speaks for itself, yet many remain difficult to discover. At the same time, customers often struggle to find creators whose craftsmanship, style, and vision match what they are looking for.

Eleo was created to bridge that gap.

This platform is more than a directory; it is a step toward making creativity easier to find, support, and celebrate. We believe that every creator deserves an opportunity to be seen, and every customer deserves access to exceptional craftsmanship.

Though Eleo is still growing, the vision remains clear: to build a trusted space where talent meets opportunity.

Thank you for being part of the journey.

— Farida Akus
Founder, Eleo
