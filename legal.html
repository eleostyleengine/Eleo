Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Welcome to Eleo. Eleo is a fashion discovery platform created to connect people with talented tailors, designers, dressmakers, and fashion creators.

Terms of Service

Our Mission: Eleo exists to help fashion creators showcase their work and help clients discover skilled professionals for their fashion needs.

Creator Profiles: Creators are responsible for ensuring that the information, portfolio images, contact details, and social media links they provide are accurate and up to date.

Review Process: Eleo reviews creator applications before approving them. Approval indicates that a creator met our requirements at the time of review.

Working With Creators: Clients are encouraged to communicate clearly with creators regarding pricing, timelines, measurements, materials, and expectations before entering into any agreement.

Platform Management: Eleo reserves the right to approve, decline, update, suspend, or remove creator listings that violate platform standards or provide misleading information.

Updates: These Terms may be updated from time to time as Eleo grows.

Privacy Policy

Eleo respects your privacy and is committed to handling information responsibly.

Information We Collect: We collect details such as business name, email, phone/WhatsApp, location, specializations, portfolio, and social media links.

How We Use Information: Used to create profiles, verify applications, improve functionality, and communicate updates.

Information Sharing: We do not sell personal information. Public profile details are visible to platform users to facilitate connections.

Profile Updates or Removal: Creators may request corrections or removal of their information by contacting support.
