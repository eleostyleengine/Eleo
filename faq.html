What is Eleo?
Eleo is a platform that helps people discover fashion creators such as tailors, designers, and dressmakers. It connects customers with skilled creators in their local area.
Is Eleo free to use?
Yes. Eleo is free for customers to browse creators. Creators may also join for free in this initial version.
How do I find a tailor on Eleo?
Go to the “Find Creators” page, browse the listings, and click on any creator to view their profile and contact them.
How do I contact a creator?
Each creator profile includes contact details such as phone number or WhatsApp for direct communication.
Can I join Eleo as a tailor or designer?
Yes. Creators can join Eleo by filling out the “Join Eleo” form. Once submitted, they will be added to the platform listing.
Can creators update their information later?
In the current version, updates are not yet automated. This feature will be added in future updates.
Does Eleo handle payments?
No. Eleo does not handle payments at this stage. Customers communicate and transact directly with creators.
Is Eleo available everywhere?
Eleo starts locally and will gradually expand to more regions over time.
How do I report a problem?
You can contact Eleo through the Contact page if you experience any issue with a creator or the platform configuration.
What kind of creators can join Eleo?
Tailors, fashion designers, dressmaker houses, embroidery specialists, and other verified fashion-related artisanal creators.
Will Eleo add more features in the future?
Yes. Eleo will grow into a more advanced platform with search filters, automated creator accounts, and enhanced discovery tools for creators and customers.

Eleo is still growing. Thank you for being part of its early journey.
