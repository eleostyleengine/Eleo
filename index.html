What is Eleo?

Fashion is personal. Finding the right creator shouldn't be difficult.

Eleo was created to help people discover talented fashion creators and connect directly with them. Whether you're looking for a tailor, a dressmaker, or a designer, Eleo makes local talent easier to find and support.
